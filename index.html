IAnL

Hello, I am IAnL (日日安), a university student and illustrator based in Kaohsiung, Taiwan. I specialize in digital illustration, with a strong focus on Blue Archive fan art and original concepts. While I am continuously dedicated to refining my craft and learning new techniques, my ultimate goal is to pursue professional opportunities in the industry. I aspire to collaborate on game art, novel covers, and character design commissions in the future.
